Perspective

Designathon Finalist

Perspective is an app that connects new and experienced designers from all over the world based on art which allows designers to portray their work anonymously.

Research / UI

Challange

“How might we connect creative students all over the world on one platform free of bias”

    • Problem

    A platform that helps design students break out of their shell, share freely without the fear of being judged from their race, sex, or location

    Concept

    Campfire is an experience for older adults that makes it easier for them to connect to one another by sharing experiences and stories to the people living in the same area using the location. It also allows the people to view the story live and comment as well. We also got an opportunity to interview seniors and get feedback as well.

    Target Audience

    Creative students

    Ideation

    The problems we wanted to tackle was the language barrier, removable of accidental bias. Inclusion of designers at different levels and experiences from different worlds. The ability to explore and see the different ways students present their creativity that is influenced by their culture

    Pain Points

    - Shyness and fear of rejection
    - Judgement on their appearance, sex, location, race or education
    - Having their art taken

    Solution

    - Users post their work anonymously: no identity on the image posting
    - Ability to spin the globe to view content from different part of the world randomly
    - Filtering system to view specific design all over the world

    Experience

    Social Anxiety can lead into hiding work from the world. In order to combat it, taking small steps and posting anonymously may help break out of that shell

    Platform

    Why this Platform?
    It's accessible and familiar. Students are often on their phones as they're on the go

    Prevention of Art Theft
    In addition, it's made more difficult to obtain a photo through an app (as the photo size will be compressed and small). A lower resolution of an image would be more difficult to steal and resell.

    Navigation

    - Ability to choose country
    - Users can view, like and post images
    - Account will have an option to keep it anonymous

    Home Page
    - The globe is interactive: spins and will land on a country and jumps into design in that specific country
    - Main goal of the app is to explore

    Feed
    - The globe is at the top, user has the ability to pull down globe and spin again
    - Usernames are hidden, unless post is clicked upon
    - Users are only to like, as it's anonymous and commenting anonymously may create issues

    Profile
    - Photo grid system as it's familiar, and photos are big enough to see from a glance. The photo may open bigger once clicked upon
    - Three main functions: My posts, liked & upload

    Reflection
    - Developing a concept that is a possibility that will remove bias opinions based on username, gender and some cases country (if they're not the globe)
    - Time constraint of two hours to design left certain details missed
    - Considered how to work in a team of four in a very short time span to concept, design, and coordinate to meet the deadline
